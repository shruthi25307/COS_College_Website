Eligibility

Admission Through TNEA

Admissions are conducted through Tamil Nadu Engineering Admissions (TNEA) based on Class 12 Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry marks.

Passed 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics.

Admission through TNEA counselling conducted by the Government of Tamil Nadu.

Minimum eligibility as per Anna University & TNEA norms.

Scholarships available for meritorious students.

Lateral Entry admissions available for Diploma holders directly into the 2nd year.

Admission Process

Four Steps to Enrolment

01

Register for TNEA

Complete your TNEA registration online and submit the required academic details through the official counselling portal.

02

Certificate Verification

Verify your Class 12 mark sheets, community certificates, and other required documents as per TNEA guidelines.

03

Choice Filling & Allotment

Select your preferred engineering programs and participate in TNEA counselling for seat allotment based on rank and availability.

04

Admission Confirmation

Report to COS, complete fee payment, and confirm your admission to begin your engineering journey.
